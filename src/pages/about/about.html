Our Smart Logic

We aren't just a taxi service. We use data to make intercity travel cheaper for you and more profitable for drivers.

💰

One-Way Optimization

We match your one-way trip with a return passenger. This allows us to offer you a 25% discount in PKR while ensuring the driver doesn't return with an empty car.

🚗

Driver Delay Mitigation

If your assigned driver is delayed by more than 15 minutes, our system automatically pings the next closest "Pro" driver to take over your route instantly.

⏰

No-Show Protection

We respect our drivers' time. If a user doesn't arrive within 15 minutes of the scheduled time, a small wait-fee is charged to compensate the driver's fuel and time.
